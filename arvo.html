Overview
What is Arvo?

Arvo! is a comedic story-driven exploration game about driving around the Australian outback in your ute and delivering packages for the quirky robot inhabitants of the mysterious Exclusion Zone.

What Did I Do?

Arvo! is developed by two people at Chronoboros Games, an indie studio co-founded by me and Frazer Carpenter (AKA Dolph). Arvo! was my first major project after uni, and I have been deeply involved in all aspect of development since its conception.

My contributions to this project include:

3D Art

- Almost all major 3D assets and texturing. E.g. Car, Environment Assets, Enemy Models, Prop Assets.

2D Art & Designs

- Characters Designs

- UI assets

- Promotional Art

Designing + Programming Major Gameplay Systems

- Driving and Car Physics

- Quest and Dialogue System

- Delivery and Physics Prop System

- Car Upgrades and Environmental Effects

UI & Menus

- All UI menus and elements
